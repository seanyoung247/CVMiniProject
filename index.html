About me

What do I do?

Like all Grouches, my mission in life is to be as miserable and grouchy as possible, and pass that feeling on to everyone else. I have a great deal of expertise and experience that I bring to making everyone as miserable as possible.

How do I do it?

I ensure to make people as unwelcome as groucherly possible. If they knock on my trash can, I always greet them with a snarl and where possible a light insult. If they stop coming to see me, I also ensure to try and make them feel bad about that as well. I find it helps add to the intruder experience.
